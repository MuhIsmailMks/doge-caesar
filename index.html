How To Buy

Step 1

Install a wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet, and create a new wallet. Store your seed phrase securely and never share it.

Step 2

Buy ETH from an exchange (such as Binance or Coinbase), then send it to your wallet address on the Ethereum Mainnet.

Step 3

Go to uniswap.org, click "Launch App", then click "Connect Wallet" and select MetaMask or Trust Wallet. Make sure the network is set to Ethereum.

Step 4

Use Uniswap to exchange ETH for $CAESAR, Set the correct slippage, then click "Swap" to complete the transaction.
